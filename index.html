Privacy policy

Last updated July 2026 · Applies to the Reembr website and app.

Summary

Reembr is built so that where your places are, and the checks that notice when you leave, stay on your phone. This website is a marketing site: it collects only what you choose to send us — a waitlist email or a support message — plus basic, privacy-respecting usage analytics.

The app: data on your device

The app stores your saved places and their names, where they are, their reminder areas, whether they’re switched on, availability schedules, linked checklists, reminder preferences, and app settings — all on your device.

The app: data synced with your account

If you sign in, we may store a sign-in ID, your email, display name, profile photo, sign-in methods, plan status, checklist templates, checklist items, and safe app settings. Where your places are, where you’ve been, and your comings and goings never sync.

The app: location data

In this release we do not store where your places are, their reminder areas, your location readings, or your comings and goings on our servers. Location permission is used on your device to help decide when to show a reminder.

This website: waitlist & beta sign-ups

When you join the waitlist or ask to be notified about the Android beta, we store the email address you enter (and which button it came from) so we can email you at launch. We don’t sell it or use it for unrelated marketing.

This website: support messages

When you use the support form, we receive the topic, the message you write, and — if you provide one — your email address, so we can reply. Please don’t include sensitive personal information you don’t want us to hold.

This website: analytics & cookies

We use Google Analytics and Firebase Analytics to understand which parts of the site people use (for example, which buttons get pressed) so we can improve it. These set cookies or similar identifiers and collect standard technical data like device type, approximate region, and pages viewed. We don’t use them to identify you personally.

Notifications

In the app, notifications remind you to run a checklist. Delivery depends on your device, operating system, permissions, and battery settings.

Payments

If you buy a paid plan, payment details are handled by the payment provider. We may receive plan status, subscription identifiers, renewal state, and entitlement information. We do not receive full card details.

Data retention

Local app data stays on your device until you delete it, uninstall, or clear app data. Waitlist emails, support messages, and account data are kept while they’re useful for the purpose collected or as needed for legal, billing, security, and operational reasons.

Your choices

You can change notification and location permissions in system settings, edit or delete places and checklists in the app, and ask us to remove your waitlist email or support message via the support form. You can limit analytics through your browser settings or an ad/analytics blocker.

Children

Reembr is not intended for children under the age required to use online account services in their region.

Changes

We may update this policy as the product changes. If a change materially affects how location-related data is handled, we’ll provide clear notice in the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the support form on this site or the support option in the app.
